Tesla Model 3

Model 3 has been designed to attain the highest safety ratings in every category, Model 3 achieves 220 miles of range while starting at only 35,000 USD before incentives. Model 3 is a smaller, simpler, more affordable electric car. Although it is Tesla's newest vehicle, Model 3 is not “Version 3” or the most advanced Tesla. Like Model S, it is designed to be the safest car in its class. The Standard Interior is A 15” touchscreen display,Dual zone climate control system,FM/Internet streaming radio,Textile seating and Center console with open storage and two USB ports. The Safety in the Model 3 is automatic emergency braking and collision avoidance,Eight airbags,Electronic stability and traction control.
